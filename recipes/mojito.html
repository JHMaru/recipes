Mojito

Description

A refreshing summer drink originating from the carribean. This drink is made from rum, however, what makes the drink a classic is the refreshing mint and lime addition to the palate. Nothing beats a sweet citrus and mint rum cocktail to cool off with on a hot day. Except one with ice...

Ingredients

  • Rum
  • Ice
  • Club Soda
  • Limes
  • Simple Syrup
  • Fresh sprigs of Mint

Steps

  1. Lightly muddle your desired amount of mint in the bottom of a collins glass. Do so gently, as to not tear the leaves as you express the oils.
  2. fill the glass with small ice cubes, and add three quarters of an ounce of simple syrup
  3. Add an ounce of lime juice to the glass
  4. add two ounces of Rum
  5. Top the glass with club soda and lightly incorporate the ingredients together with a bar spoon
  6. Garnish with a fresh sprig of mint
