Old Fashioned

Description

This caramel coloured imbibement serves as a flourish for any fine pour of Bourbon, or an excellent candidate for dressing up a lacking pour with the essence of fresh fruit and classic aromatic bitters. The pinch of sugar and added aroma of citrus extends the palate and sophistication of most Whiskeys to classic heights. While bourbon is the traditional spirit of choice for this beverage, a nice american rye is a welcome substitution.

Ingredients

  • Bourbon
  • Ice
  • Simple Syrup
  • Angostora Bitters
  • Peels of Lemon and Orange
  • Cherry

Steps

  1. Mix two ounces bourbon, a quarter ounce of simple syrup, and two dashes of bitters in a rocks glass
  2. Add a large ice cube, and garnish with a cherry and twist of lemon
  3. Rim the glass with the oils of an orange peel
