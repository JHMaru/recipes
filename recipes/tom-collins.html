Tom Collins

Description

A carbonated and boozy take on lemonade. While Gin is not currently at the height of polularity, the Tom Collins is a shade of cool that never goes out of style.

Ingredients

  • Gin
  • Ice
  • Club Soda
  • Lemons
  • Simple Syrup

Steps

  1. Add an ounce of lemon juice to the glass
  2. Fill the glass with small ice cubes, and add three quarters of an ounce of simple syrup
  3. Add two ounces of Gin
  4. Top the glass with club soda and lightly incorporate the ingredients together with a bar spoon
  5. Garnish with a lemon wheel
